Our Story

Seaport Cellars was born from a passion for wine and a dream decades in the making. Our founder embarked on his winemaking journey to create exceptional, small-batch wines in the heart of Carlton, Oregon.

Our name is a nod to the maritime spirit and the hard-working ports that define the character of the Pacific Northwest. We believe in letting the quality of the grapes speak for itself, with minimal intervention to bring forth the truest expression of the fruit.

Our first vintage, a 2024 Grenache from the renowned Lowry Vineyard, marks the beginning of our story. We are thrilled to share our wine with you and look forward to the journey ahead.
